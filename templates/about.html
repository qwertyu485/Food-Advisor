Welcome to FoodAdvisor

FoodAdvisor is your personal nutrition assistant, designed to help you make informed decisions about your diet and nutrition. Our platform leverages the comprehensive USDA FoodData Central database to provide accurate and detailed nutritional information for thousands of foods.

Our Mission

We believe that understanding what you eat is the first step towards a healthier lifestyle. FoodAdvisor makes it easy to look up nutrition facts, track your meals, and make better food choices every day.

Features

  • Food Search: Search our extensive database for detailed nutrition information
  • Meal Calculator: Track the nutritional content of your meals
  • Search History: Quickly access your previous searches
  • User Accounts: Save your preferences and personalize your experience

Data Source

Our nutritional data comes from the USDA FoodData Central, a trusted source maintained by the U.S. Department of Agriculture. This ensures that you get accurate, up-to-date information for all your nutritional queries.
